Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

The site name will be Colorado Springs Chamber of Commerce because it represents the city I have selected to base my business commerce site about.

Purpose

This site purpose is to encourage businesses to join the Colorado Springs Chamber of Commerce. The website will show them community events and local resources available to their businesses, if they choose to join. The Colorado Springs Chamber of Commerce will spotlight local businesses, history and events, drawing new consumers and tourists to the community's businesses. The site will offer information on the different tiers of membership and this will allow each business to select the best option for their entity right on the website.

Scenarios

  • What networking resources and events will be available to help my business grow?
  • What are some of Colorado Springs historical attractions?
  • What has the population growth been like in Colorado Springs over the past 2 years?
  • How many local businesses participate in Colorado Springs Chamber of Commerce?
